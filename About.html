Who are we?

Our Mission

Here at NYP Distance, our mission is to inspire runners of all levels to challenge themselves through distance-based goals. Whether you're aiming for your first 5K or training for a marathon, we provide a supportive and motivating environment to help you go the extra mile. Our focus is on personal growth, endurance, and fostering a community of runners who push each other to be better every day.

Our Values

Commitment

Progress takes time, and every step counts. We show up consistently, stay disciplined, and hold ourselves accountable — because success is earned, not given.

Endurance

We build both physical and mental resilience — pushing through fatigue, setbacks, and challenges with determination. Endurance helps us go the extra mile, both on the track and in life.

Community

Running is more than an individual sport — we train, support, and grow together. Every teammate plays a role in building a strong, inclusive, and encouraging environmen

Why Us?

At NYP Distance, we're not just a running group - we're a supportive family that encourages everyone to achieve their personal best.
