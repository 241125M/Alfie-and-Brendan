Who are we?

Here at NYP Distance, we adhere to a set of values that guides us towards our mission.

Our Mission

Here at NYP Distance, our mission is to inspire runners of all levels to challenge themselves through distance-based goals. Whether you're aiming for your first 5K or training for a marathon, we provide a supportive and motivating environment to help you go the extra mile. Our focus is on personal growth, endurance, and fostering a community of runners who push each other to be better every day.

Our Values

We follow 3 core values that guides our way.

Commitment

We commit to our goals and each other.

Endurance

We believe in pushing through barriers.

Community

We support and uplift each other.

Why us?

At NYP Distance, we're not just a running group - we're a supportive family that encourages everyone to achieve their personal best.
